    Bio-Vital

    Bio-Vital is a unique nutritional solution formulated to support underweight birds. It improves growth performance, supports immunity, optimizes digestion, and boosts reproductive development—ultimately enhancing flock profitability.

    • BENEFITS
    • COMPOSITION
    • DOSAGE
    • Accelerates Weight gain in growers.
    • Makes bird more resistant to diseases.
    • Increases non-specific immunity.
    • Reduces early chick mortality.
    • Reduces FCR.
    • Improves digestion & absorption.
    • Keeps intestine free from lesions.
    • Helps in the development of reproductive organs & ovary.
    • Helps in the onset of lay on time.
    • Helps attain peak production faster and helps maintain it for a longer time.
    • Improves the overall flock profitability.
    • Ensures weight gain when administered to culls.
    Enzymes Protease, Lipase, Hemicellulase, Amylase, Phytase and Xylanase.
    Amino Acids Leucine, Valine, Threonine, Glycine, Tryptophan and Arginine.
    Immunostimulants 1-3, 1-6, Beta-glucans and Beta-carotene.
    Prebiotic Fructooligosaccharides, Xylooligosaacharides Lactulose and Inulin.
    Prebiotic Propretary Isolates of Lactobacillus, Bacilus and Saccharomyces genus. Plus Microbial Derived Growth Factors & Enzyme Activators.

    Grower: 500 gms/ ton of feed from day 1 up to 8th week and 1 Kg/ ton of feed from 9th week to 18th week

    Culls: 1Kg/ ton of feed 4-6 weeks before marketing.

    Presentation

    Powder: 5Kg
